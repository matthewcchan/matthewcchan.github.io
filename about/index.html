Hello! My name is Matthew.

I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate in the Shukla Group in the Department of Chemical and Biomolecular Enginering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research involves using computational methods, primarily molecular dynamics simulations, to investigate complex dynamics of membrane transport proteins.

Before my pursuing my Ph.D., I obtained my bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering and Applied Mathematics at The University of Alabama at Birmingham. I conducted my undergradute research with Dr. Margaret A. Johnson in the Department of Chemistry investigating functional mechanisms of poly(ADP-ribose) polymerases and structural determination using solution NMR.
